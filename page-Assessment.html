Timeline & Milestones

The timeline to move from on-premise EDW to the Cloud contains key milestones. A typical cloud migration can take anywhere from 3 to 6 months. The timeline includes a cloud assessment, a proof of concept migration of a lower environment and the migration of the production system. Additional milestones can be added for Architecture Modernization, Re-engineering data ingestion and Business Intelligence applications

Quick Assessment

DataLake Labs provides a Quick Assessment of your existing EDW Environment.
This assessment is centered around providing you complexity analysis, overall cost of migrating and running in the cloud, and a high level approach and timeline. Typically this process is free to the customer and involves time and resources from the customer IT and Business departments.

Detailed Assessment

The DataLake Labs Detailed Assessment goes beyond the first week of interactions and includes a migration workshop, a proof of concept migration to remove any surprises on the production system migration. This assessment includes many components related to technical architecture, detailed costs, migration blockers and critical success factors.
