About us :

We are 100% virtual. We are a network of individuals with over 1 million hours of combined technical work experience in the Data Warehousing and Cloud space.

We employ the best and the brightest talent available to undertake the most complex technical and business challenges. Our network of professionals include legacy SME's, traditional data warehouse and ETL implementers, cloud solution architects and engineers, and business analysts who bring industry experience along with deep technical experience.

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in San Francisco with a global remote workforce, DataLake Labs is a no "BS" as in Bad Services consulting firm that provides superior professional services at rates below most System Integrators. We keep our costs low and provide outcome-based consulting. You don't pay us until you achieve your desired result.
