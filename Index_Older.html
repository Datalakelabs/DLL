Outcome Based Systems Integrator

We are an Outcome-based, End-to-End Cloud Systems Integrator. Our key focus is to help you in your Journey to your chosen Public Cloud. Our charging model is dependant upon pre-determined milestones. If there is only one milestone, we do not charge you until that milestone is reached. We can also come up with other creative ways of charging for a Migration: For example: a portion or all of our consulting fees can be recovered from reducing consumption of your existing system.

Focus: Data Warehouse Migration Assessment

Use Cases

We used DataLake Labs to help us migrate our out of support on-premise Teradata 14.10 Database to Vantage on AWS. The process and planning around the migration was handled with precision. Post Cutover and catchup, our ETL and BI users didn't even realize they were connecting to the cloud

DataLake Labs current system assessement of our end-of-life on-premise NETEZZA MAKO system workloads gave us insight into our 12 year old legacy data warhouse. We used this knowledge to do some housekeeping as we retired old workloads and migrated the necessary ones to Redshift

After evaluating multiple SI partners:Rackspace, Navisite and Cognizant, we decided to go forward with DataLake Labs because they truly understood our requirements and how migration cost was major factor for us. We really appreciated the help they provided our organization savings of $5m

We had several failed attempts at migrating our Oracle Rack to RDS. Migrating data and database in Cloud is the easy part. What we didn't realize was all the interfaces that needed to be resolved. We had shared mounts, complex dependencies between on-premises systems.

DataLake Labs have it right when it comes to Managed Services. They understand that it is not about increasing or reducing consumption, but about managing consumption. It's not just montoring systems and resolving tickets. it is about reducing our TCO, so we don't pay extra just for peace of mind
