Our Organization and Promise

DataLake Labs is a global, Migrate-to-Cloud Platform-as-a-Service Consulting Company built to migrate coporate legacy data warehouse systems to a modern analytics platform in the cloud. We have only one goal in mind: to ensure our customers are successful in their migration to the cloud, are able to achieve desired cost savings and provide a best-in-class environment and experience for their end-users
Our expertise is spread over all aspects of Data Warehousing: Mainframe, Legacy Databases, Traditional ETL tools, Modern Data Pipeline Platforms, Columnar Databases, MPP Databases, Data Prep Applications, Business Intelligence Applications, Cloud Security and Infrastructure and Data Migration and Conversion Tools. We bring over 150-years of combined experience to the table to help you in your journey to Public Cloud.
