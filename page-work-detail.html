Managed Services Consumption Model

  • See how we can save you $200,000 a year in Managed Services
  • DataLake Labs
  • Services
  • Use Case

A Traditional Managed Services Model includes monitoring and managing your system and providing high or medium touch support for your on-premise EDW. One of the limitations of the traditional model is that there is no real opportunity to save cost by reducing consumption.

Cloud Consumption costs need to be managed to avoid a end-of-month surprise in the form a large bill from the cloud provider. Even though most cloud vendors provide tools to monitor consumption, there are additional measures that can be taken to lower consumption, especially during times when users are not logging on. Cloud Managed service providers should actively scale systems down to provide you monthly savings. In a typical example, just dialing a 2-node Teradata System down from 7pm to 9am on Friday's can save $66,885 annually. In addition Managed Services team can collaorate with your IT department to build charge-back models for business groups that want month-end reporting to complete on-time.

Establish your baseline cost to be paid to the Cloud provider at 100% of the system. With our Managed Services model, we can collaborate with you to determine your cloud cost run-rate: so you have no additional checks to write. Let us manage and maintain your Data Warehouse and taking the measures to reduce unnecessary consumption.

  • Nominal yearly fees for Managed Services
  • Managed Services uses an AWS recovery Model to fill in gaps in Total Revenue
  • High Value Consumption Model - Minimal Impact to End Users
  • No change orders, no hidden fees. You pay AWS hosting and Teradata License Fees and Usage
  • We actively manage and monitor your system and scale down as needed
